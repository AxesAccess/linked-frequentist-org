Get clarity on your professional network

Analyze your LinkedIn™ connections to understand who you’re connected with, stay in touch with people who matter, and expand your network deliberately.

Get Started

Who are you connected with?

Import your LinkedIn™ data export and instantly see the structure of your professional network: countries, industries, positions, and connection dates. Get a clear picture that LinkedIn’s feed never shows.

Powerful filters and visualization

Search by name, company, or position with regex support. Filter by country, connection date, latest private message, and endorsements. Visualize your network on an interactive map or cluster connections by their current position.

Take control of your interactions

LinkedIn™ decides what you see — not you. Mark your key connections as favourites, track private messages, and focus on the people that really matter to your goals.

Instead of relying on algorithms, choose who to stay in touch with, and never lose track of important professional relationships again.

Clean up your network

Over the years, your network grows — sometimes in directions that no longer match your goals. Identify inactive or outdated connections and keep your network aligned with your current interests and location.

Discover new connections

Use advanced filters to find people worth connecting with — by country, company, position, or activity level. Save hours of manual browsing and discover active professionals in your target industry or region.

You’ll soon be able to view engagement patterns — see who regularly shares ideas and participates in discussions, so you can connect with people who truly contribute to your professional field.

Custom lists, saved filters, and improved clustering algorithms to help you segment your network the way you want. We’re also adding insights based on shared interests and engagement to make it easier to identify meaningful connections.
